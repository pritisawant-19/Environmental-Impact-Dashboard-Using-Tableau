# Environmental Impact Dashboard

## Overview

The **Environmental Impact Dashboard** is an interactive tool designed to monitor and analyze environmental metrics across different regions and industries. It helps organizations and stakeholders visualize key sustainability indicators, including carbon emissions, water consumption, energy usage, and recycling rates. The dashboard enables data-driven decisions to improve environmental performance and sustainability practices.

---

## Key Features

1. **Filters**

   * **Compliance Year**: Filter data by specific years or multiple years.
   * **Industry**: Select an industry to analyze specific sector performance.
   * **Region**: Filter data based on geographical regions.

2. **Metrics**

   * **Total Carbon Emissions**: Displays the overall carbon emissions in millions (16.2M).
   * **Average Recycling Rate**: Shows the average recycling rate percentage across regions and industries (72%).
   * **Total Energy Usage**: Indicates the total energy consumption in millions (37.46M).
   * **Total Water Consumption**: Displays total water consumption across regions in millions (49.9M).

3. **Visualizations**

   * **Total Water Consumption by Region**

     * Bar chart showing water consumption values for each region (Asia, North America, Middle East, South America, Europe, Africa, Australia).
   * **Energy Usage Trends**

     * Line chart displaying trends in energy usage over time (dates shown for July 14â€“19).
   * **Recycling Rate by Region and Industry**

     * Table displaying recycling rates across industries and regions.
   * **Relationship Between Carbon Emissions and Recycling Rates**

     * Scatter plot showing correlation between carbon emissions and recycling rates.

## Data Sources

* Environmental data collected from multiple regions and industries.
* Metrics include carbon emissions, water consumption, energy usage, and recycling rates.
* Data is aggregated and normalized to allow comparisons across regions and industries.

---

## How to Use

1. **Apply Filters**

   * Select desired year(s), industry, and region from dropdown menus to view specific data.
2. **Analyze Metrics**

   * Observe the key metrics on the left panel for a quick overview.
3. **Visualize Trends**

   * Use the charts to explore trends over time and relationships between metrics.
4. **Compare Regions & Industries**

   * Analyze differences in water consumption, recycling rates, and carbon emissions across regions and industries.
